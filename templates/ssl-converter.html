Format Information
PEM Format
Base64 encoded data with headers. Most common format for certificates and private keys.
DER Format
Binary encoded data. Used in Windows and some other systems.
PKCS#7 Format
Container format that can hold multiple certificates and CRLs.
PKCS#12 Format
Password-protected container that can hold certificates and private keys.
